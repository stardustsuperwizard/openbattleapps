Units and Gear

Unit

A unit is a single entity that exists in the tabletop. Models have the following profile stats:

  • Ranged Combat (RC)
  • Close Combat (CC)
  • Physical Toughnes (PT)
  • Mental Toughness (MT)
  • Hit Points (HP)
  • Combat Save (CS)
  • Movement (MV)

Gear

Gear are tools and abilities used by models to perform combat actions against other units. Weapons have the following profile stats:/p>

  • Distance (Dst)
  • Attacks (A)
  • Strength (S)
  • Save Modifier (SM)
